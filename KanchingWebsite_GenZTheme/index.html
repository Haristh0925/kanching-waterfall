About Kanching Waterfall

Kanching Waterfall, also known as Taman Eko Rimba Kanching, is one of Malaysia’s hidden gems, located in Rawang, Selangor. This stunning recreational forest is home to seven cascading waterfalls, making it a favorite destination for nature enthusiasts, families, and adventure seekers. Surrounded by lush rainforest, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Why Visit Kanching Waterfall?

  • Seven-Tiered Waterfalls: The seven tiers offer experiences ranging from shallow pools for families to deeper pools for swimming.
  • Scenic Hiking Trails: Well-maintained jungle trails lead to breathtaking views at the higher tiers.
  • Biodiversity: Encounter wildlife like monkeys, exotic birds, and tropical plants in their natural habitat.
  • Picnic and Relaxation: Enjoy designated picnic areas surrounded by nature.
  • Instagram-Worthy Views: Capture stunning landscapes and cascading waterfalls for lasting memories.

How to Reach Kanching Waterfall

  • By Car: Located just 30 minutes from Kuala Lumpur, it’s easily accessible by car with parking available at the entrance.
  • By Public Transport: Take the KTM Komuter train to Rawang Station, then a taxi or Grab to the waterfall.

Facilities and Amenities

  • Picnic and Barbecue Areas
  • Toilets and Changing Rooms
  • Ample Parking Space
  • Guided Tours (optional)

Safety Tips

  • Wear appropriate shoes for hiking as trails can be slippery.
  • Secure your belongings from curious monkeys.
  • Bring sufficient drinking water and snacks for longer hikes.
  • Respect the environment by properly disposing of trash.

Activities

  • Hiking through scenic trails
  • Picnicking by the water
  • Swimming in natural pools
  • Photography opportunities
